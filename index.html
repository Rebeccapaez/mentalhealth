Depression symtoms and how they look

  • Masking: People with depression will to the outsider look happy and cheerful. This is masking pretending to be fine. This takes a lot of energy to do and not many will be able to see through this.
  • Sleeping habits: Ignoring the fact that humans are not meant to be sleeping for 8 hours straight. Getting too much or too little sleep is a symptom of depression.
  • Isolation: feeling so helpless but not wanting to reach out for help due to that making you feel even more guilty
